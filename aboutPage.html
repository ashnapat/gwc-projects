About the Creators

Claire Haynes

Hi! I’m Claire. I currently go to Eastside College Preparatory School in East Palo Alto. I got inspired to do this for my final project because I live in the Bay Area, and seeing the number of homeless people significantly increase breaks my heart. We can not just keep turning my head the other way, so my classmates and I decided to make our final project on something that could be useful to them.

Ashna Pattanayak

Hi! I’m Ashna. I go to Granada High School in Livermore. My favorite sport is soccer and in my free time, I like go out and hang out with my friends. I love coming to San Francisco but as I came more and more often, I kept noticing that there has been a constant increase in homelessness. With the help of Claire and Amelie, we have created a website that is meant to be resourceful and helpful to people who are homeless or in need.

Amelie Scheil

Hi! I’m Amelie. I go to San Francisco University High School. My passions include painting and drawing, playing basketball, and running track. I got into coding through Girls Who Code, and I plan on continuing after this program. This project was created to tackle the issue of homelessness in San Francisco by providing resources to those in need.
